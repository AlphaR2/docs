If you are interested in on-chain gaming here you will find like-minded individual that wants to have fun and build cool games just like yourself. We focus on building state-of-the-art infrastructure to enable what we like to call "Forever Games". Also known as "Fully on-chain games" or "Autonomous World", Forever Games are simply experiences that live entirely on a blockchain, can't be shut off, and that allow players and developers to collaborate in the creation of infinitely extendible worlds.
